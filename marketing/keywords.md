## Keyword Strategy Notes

### Search Volume Scale
- High: 1500+ monthly searches
- Medium: 500-1499 monthly searches
- Low: Under 500 monthly searches

### Difficulty Scale
- High: Many established competitors ranking
- Medium: Some competition, rankable with good content
- Low: Few competitors, easy to rank
